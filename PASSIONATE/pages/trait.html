FINDING YOUR TRAITS

It's important to recognize that your trait adjectives represent a versatile collection of words that can be tailored to suit the specific requirements of the job you're applying for. Moreover, your traits are dynamic and subject to change over time, so there's no need to feel constrained by a fixed set. Define what feels right for now.

Firstly, traits adjectives are words used to describe a person. If you do have a specific career category in mind, think about the traits required to fulfill those jobs. That will help you brainstorm some possible trait additives.

Ex: generous, approachable, punctual, detailed
