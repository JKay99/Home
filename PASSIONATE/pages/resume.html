RESUME IN TRAINING

Tailoring your resume is quiet simple once you know what to look for.

Research the Company

Familiarize yourself with the company's values, culture, and specific needs for the position you're applying for. This will help you align your resume with their expectations.

Personalize Your Objective or Summary

Customizing emphasizes how your skills and experiences are in line with the company's objectives and the job's prerequisites.

Match Keywords

Be sure to carefully review the job description and use their keywords and phrases within your resume. Doing so aids in navigating the applicant tracking systems (ATS) and showcases your alignment with the role.

Tailor Your Education

If you have any relevant courses, certifications, or extracurricular activities, incorporate them into your education section to emphasize your fit for the position.
